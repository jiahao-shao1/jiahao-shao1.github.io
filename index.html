HUGS utilizes 3D Gaussian Splatting for holistic urban scene understanding with only posed RGB image as input. The model extend 3D Gaussian Splatting to model camera exposure, optical flow, semantics and dynamic objects for holistic understanding while it could achieve real-time rendering at 100 fps.
